About

I am a data scientist who does not sit still. I move across tools, industries, and questions because that is how I am wired. I am curious about everything, comfortable with nothing staying the same.

I believe the most valuable analysis finds the gaps: in risk, in opportunity, in who is being missed by the current model. Whether that is a hospital system, a lending portfolio, or a retail supply chain, I am looking for what the data reveals that nobody has acted on yet.

I build work that is presentable, precise, and honest. I show up as a collaborator, not a yes-man. I think the best teams are built on respect in both directions.
