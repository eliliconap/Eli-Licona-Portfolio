Who Gets to go Home?

My grandmother survived COVID back in 2020. Her experience navigating the healthcare system as an elderly woman of color made me want to understand a pattern hiding in plain sight. Who gets discharged before they are truly ready, and why do many of them come back within 30 days?

This analysis uses publicly available CMS Hospital Readmission and DHHS demographic health data to examine 30-day readmission rates across Charlotte-Mecklenburg hospitals with a focus on elderly patients, Medicaid recipients, and racial minorities.
